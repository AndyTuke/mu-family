Capabilities

Drone synthesis,
rhythmically sculpted.

μ-tant is built for producers who want to push synthesis beyond static patches into evolving, rhythmically alive textures.

2-Oscillator Wavetable Drone

Two wavetable oscillators run continuously, producing a permanent drone. Independent wavetable selection and position scanning on each oscillator — sweep through timbral space for shifting harmonic content.

Core Voice

FM & Ring Modulation

X-mod routes FM and ring modulation between the two oscillators. Dial in subtle harmonic enrichment or push into metallic, clangorous territory — all while the drone continuously evolves.

X-Mod

Envelope Gate Sequencer

Up to 16 bars of step-based gating. Each step has its own customisable envelope — shape the attack from a hard chop to a slow, swelling fade. Per-step probability and loop-N-of-M gating add polyrhythmic variation.

Sequencer

Per-Step Filter & Pitch

Two filters — run them in series for deep sculpting or in parallel for split-band textures. Each step in the gate sequencer independently controls filter cutoff and pitch. Programme melodic sequences, filter sweeps, or pitch jumps — all driven by the envelope-gated drone.

Step Control

8 Modulation Sources

Eight MSEG or stepped-sequence modulators per voice, routable to any parameter — wavetable position, oscillator pitch, filter cutoff, x-mod depth. The synthesis is never static unless you want it to be.

Modulation

Mixer & Sidechain

Identical mixer to μ-clid — three sends, two master inserts, sidechain on every channel. Any voice ducks any other, or from an external sidechain signal. Use a μ-clid kick to pump a μ-tant drone pad for classic electronic textures.
