Capabilities

Everything you need to go deep.

μ-clid is built for producers who want to go beyond step sequencers and into genuinely generative territory.

Three-Layer Euclidean Engine

Each rhythm runs three independent euclidean sequences. Layers A and B combine through a logic gate to produce hit triggers. Layer C accents those hits. All three can be different lengths for polyrhythmic results that evolve across bars.

Core Engine

Pattern Ring Display

Each rhythm renders as a live donut ring — arc segments represent steps, active hits glow with colour. Euclid A, B, and the accent layer each occupy their own concentric ring. The sidebar shows all 8 rhythms at a glance.

Visualisation

Boolean Logic Gates

AND, OR, XOR, A Only, B Only — five modes combine euclidean layers A and B. XOR fires where the patterns don't overlap, generating rhythms no human would programme.

Pattern Logic

Full Voice Chain

Every hit fires a sample through pitch, filter, and amplitude — each with a full ADSR envelope. Choose from multiple filter algorithms. A per-rhythm insert FX slot lets you sculpt the sound before the mixer.

Audio Engine

8 Modulation Sources

Eight MSEG or stepped-sequence modulators per rhythm, routable to any parameter. Stack multiple targets per source for complex motion and variation built directly into the sound.

Modulation

Mixer & Sidechain

Full mixer with three sends (selectable effect, dedicated delay, reverb), two master inserts, and sidechain input on every channel. Any rhythm can duck any other.

Mixing
